    Senior Honors

    You are encouraged to work toward Latin Honors (cum laude, magna, summa). To qualify for Latin Honors in the major by thesis, you must complete special literary research and prepare and orally defend an Honors thesis, which is judged by an Honors faculty committee. To qualify for Latin Honors by course work, you must enroll in 1. Italian 4951 (Senior Honors, fall of the senior year); 2. Italian 495 (Senior Honors, spring of the senior year). Recommendations for Honors are based on your performance and the quality of the thesis, plus your cumulative grade point average. (See Departmental Guidelines for more information about Latin Honors.)
